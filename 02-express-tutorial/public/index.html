Murder Mystery in Candyland

The sugary streets of Candyland were usually peaceful, but tonight, something was terribly wrong. The grand gumdrop clock struck midnight, and a scream echoed through Licorice Lane.

Detective Fudge arrived at the scene—a crime in the heart of Peppermint Plaza. King Caramel was found lifeless, his golden crown resting beside him in a pool of melted chocolate. The suspects? The conniving Lord Licorice, the sweet yet secretive Lady Lollipop, and the ever-mysterious Gumdrop Twins.

As Detective Fudge examined the clues—a half-eaten candy cane, a trail of jellybean footprints—he realized this was no ordinary case. The sweetness of Candyland masked a bitter truth, and he had until sunrise to uncover the culprit before the entire kingdom crumbled.
